# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2026

WorkoutTracker respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app
collects and how it is used.

## Data We Collect
- Workout data you enter (exercises, sets, reps, weights, notes).
- Body metrics you enter (weight, measurements, notes, photos if you choose).
- App preferences stored on your device (for example, unit settings).

## How We Use Data
- To provide app features such as tracking, history, charts, and export.
- To display your data within the app.

## Data Storage
- All data is stored locally on your device.
- The app does not upload or store your data remotely.
- Only you can access your data on your device.

## Photos
- If you add photos, they are stored locally on your device.

## Analytics and Tracking
- The app does not use third-party analytics or tracking.

## Data Sharing
- The app does not share your data with third parties.
- If you use Export, files are generated locally and shared only by you.

## Your Choices
- You can delete your data from within the app.
- You can change unit settings at any time.
